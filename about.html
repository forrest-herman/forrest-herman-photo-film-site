About Me

Hey, I'm Forrest

I love nature, road trips, and standing on the ocean coast with salt in the air and the caress of a breeze on your face. And most of all, I love creating visuals and marking memories with my camera in hand.

I’m a storyteller, and I want to tell your story as unique-to-you as possible. If you’re not as comfortable posing in front of the camera — that’s fine! Let’s find an activity you love, and capture candid pictures instead. Everyone has something that makes them smile, makes them laugh. For me, photography is about capturing people in their element. Capturing what makes you—you.

Your story is unique, and I can’t wait to tell it.
